Our Services

With over 30 years of experience, we specialize in all types of clothing alterations and repairs. Bring in any garment, and we'll work with you personally to ensure perfect fit and satisfaction. Our commitment is helping you look and feel your best.

Pants and Jeans

  • Hemming and original hem
  • Taking in or letting out (for better waist fit)
  • Crotch (Up and Down), around hips (taking in and out)(
  • Tapering lined or unlined pants

Shirts and Blouses

  • Sleeve adjustments
  • Shoulder and arm fitting
  • Taking in or letting out
  • Shorten and Lengthening

Jackets and Coats

  • Sleeve adjustments
  • Shoulder and arm fitting
  • Taking in or letting out
  • Shorten and Lengthening

Wedding Dress

  • 1:1 consultation to have the dress fit perfectly for your special day

Dresses and Skirts

  • Hemming
  • Taking in or letting out
  • 1:1 consultation to have the dress fit perfectly

Repairs and Miscellaneous

  • Buttons (replacing or making buttonholes for shirts, jackets, etc.)
  • Zippers
  • Fasteners (replacing and fixing)
  • Velcro patches and patching
  • Tears and rips
  • Extra holes for belt
  • Replace and repair elastic bands
